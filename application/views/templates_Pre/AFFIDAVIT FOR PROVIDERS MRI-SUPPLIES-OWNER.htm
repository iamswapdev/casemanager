3. I have personal knowledge of Avir Surgical Supplies Inc routine office procedures and business policies concerning the generation and mailing of the bills. The following is a description of our routine office procedures and business policies concerning the generation and mailing of the bills. The prescription(s) for the durable medical equipment along with the patients insurance and personal information is provided to Avir Surgical Supplies Inc by the patient. Part of our procedure, better described below, requires a patient to complete and execute the assignment of benefits form, in order to assign the patients rights for payment under the patients no-fault benefits to Avir Surgical Supplies Inc Once same is completed and executed, the prescribed durable medical equipment is received by the patient and patients insurance and personal information are entered into the computer software, a bill is electronically created by Avir Surgical Supplies Inc billing personnel. It is Avir Surgical Supplies Incs regular business practice to obtain the information recorded in the bills from the above-described documents. It is Avir Surgical Supplies Incs regular course of business to train its billing personnel to read and interpret the above-described documents. Durable medical equipment is billed in accordance with the Medicaid fee schedule. However, if the durable medical equipment is not listed within the Medicaid fee schedule, the equipment is then billed by calculating the acquisition cost to the provider plus 50%, according to the No-Fault Insurance fee schedule, Regulation 83, Part E(b)(1). The bill is then printed out, copied, packaged in the envelope (addressed to the carrier, as printed on the bill) along with applicable verification material including but not limited to the prescription for the durable medical equipment provided to the assignor and invoice, verifying the purchase of the durable medical equipment provided to the assignor by Avir Surgical Supplies Inc. After is envelope is sealed and the applicable postage charges are paid, it is mailed to the applicable insurance carrier in accordance with the mailing procedure described below and same is performed within the statutory timeframes in effect at the time bills are generated and mailed. The practices and procedures described in this paragraph were in effect and followed at the time services in dispute were provided to Faina Kreyerman, the assignor in this action and at the time the bills were generated and mailed to Tristate Insurance Company for the services in dispute provided to Faina Kreyerman, the assignor in this action. It is Avir Surgical Supplies Inc.s general business practice to mail a copy of the properly executed assignment of benefits form along with the patients foremost bill. The above described practice was in effect and followed at the time services in dispute were provided to Faina Kreyerman, the assignor in this action and at the time the bills were generated and mailed to Tristate Insurance Company for the services in dispute provided to Faina Kreyerman, the assignor in this action.

A USPS agent receives the envelopes containing the bills, verifies that the addressee indicated in each entry matches the addressee on the corresponding envelope, collects and places for mailing the envelopes containing the bills in discussions, and then stamps Avir Surgical Supplies Inc.s book on the page or pages listing the bills that were mailed on that day.
